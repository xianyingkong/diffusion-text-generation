Diffusion-Driven Shakespeare is a diffusion-based conditional generative model that is trained to generate text in the style of Shakespeare's. This project is inspired by the recent breakthrough in continuous diffusion for natural language. Our model follows the architecture of DiffuSeq (Gong et al. 2023), the first diffusion-based Seq2Seq model, with modifications on training and fine-tuning techniques. The goal of our project is to demonstrate the ability of diffusion models in learning complex text representations and sentence structures.
